Quick Reference

APLS weight formulas

  • 0–12 months: (0.5 × months) + 4
  • 1–5 years: (2 × age) + 8
  • 6–12 years: (3 × age) + 7

Compressions

  • Rate: 100–120/min
  • Depth: ⅓ AP diameter (~4 cm infant, ~5 cm child)
  • Ratio: 15:2 with airway, 30:2 lay rescuer

Reversible causes — 4 Hs & 4 Ts

  • Hypoxia · Hypovolaemia · Hypo/Hyperkalaemia · Hypothermia
  • Tension pneumothorax · Tamponade · Toxins · Thromboembolism
For clinical decision support only. Based on APLS 6th edition / Resuscitation Council UK guidance. Verify every dose against BNFc and local protocols before administration.
