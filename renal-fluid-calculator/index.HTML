Clinical Guidance

Holliday-Segar: 100 mL/kg/day (first 10 kg) + 50 mL/kg/day (next 10 kg) + 20 mL/kg/day (>20 kg)
Deficit: Weight (kg) × % dehydration × 10
Maintenance cap: 2000 mL/day for children >40 kg or obese
5% dehydration: Clinical dehydration, no shock — replace over 24–48h
10% dehydration: Shocked / severe — consider IV resuscitation bolus first
Potassium caution: Use K⁺-containing fluids with caution at rates above maintenance
Reference: NICE NG29 (2015) · RCH Melbourne Clinical Guidelines
For clinical decision support only. Always verify against local guidelines.
